Heatflow.org

A repository for data and models relevant to thermal studies of the Earth. including ThermoGlobe, the latest revision of the global heat flow database. Here you will find estimates of heat flow, thermal conductivity and heat generation as well as quality indicators and additional data relevant to interpreting thermal data.

ThermoGlobe is a revised version of the Global Heat Flow Database by Dr Derrick Hasterok and Sam Jennings from the University of Adelaide, Australia. It contains {{ db.heatflow__count }} individual heat flow estimates as well as {{ db.conductivity__count }} thermal conductivity, {{ db.heatgeneration__count }} heat generation and {{ db.temperature__count }} temperature data. Data are sourced from {{ db.reference__count }} individual references spanning over 100 years of heat flow research from {{ db.reference__year__min }} to {{db.reference__year__max}}.
